Welcome to Calm New Tab

Thank you for installing Calm New Tab! This extension replaces your new tab page with a clean, distraction-free experience focused on what matters most to you.

Features

RSS Feed Integration

Stay updated with your favorite websites using our integrated RSS reader. Add any RSS feed to customize your reading experience.

Beautiful Backgrounds

Each new tab comes with a stunning daily background image to inspire you.

Quick Access

Easily access your most visited sites without cluttering your view.

Customizable

Configure your new tab experience just the way you like it through the settings page.

Getting Started

Your new tab page is ready to use! To customize your experience:

  1. Open a new tab to see Calm New Tab in action
  2. Click the settings icon in the top right corner
  3. Add your favorite RSS feeds
  4. Configure display preferences
Open New Tab Go to Settings

Calm New Tab respects your privacy. We don't collect any personal data or browsing history.
